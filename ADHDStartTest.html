ADHD Test

What is ADHD?

ADHD stands for attention deficit hyperactivity disorder. It is a neurodevelopmental condition that affects both children and adults. Individuals with ADHD find it difficult to focus on tasks, stay organized, and resist impulseful urges. Although ADHD is relatively common, its symptoms and their level of severity can vary depending on the person, which often makes it hard to diagnose.

Is This Accurate?

This online diagnosis test is not 100% accurate as ADHD symptoms are not definitive. That being said, you can use this as a self-screening tool to track your behaviour and hint towards a possible diagnosis. If you think you have symptoms of ADHD, trained medical professionals such as doctors or mental health specialists can help you identify and proceed with next steps.

Depression Test

What is Depression?

Have you been feeling down decently but can’t pinpoint why exactly? Well, the reason may be because of depression; a common mental disorder. Depression varies from person to person, and its effects can be long or short term These effects can also drastically change a person’s ability to live a normal life, and the causes of depression range from social, psychological, and biological factors. It can also be caused by life events such as losing a close friend.

Is This Accurate?

This online diagnosis test is not 100% accurate compared to a doctor That being said, you can use this as a self-screening tool to track your behaviour and hint towards a possible diagnosis. If you think you have symptoms of depression, trained medical professionals such as doctors or mental health specialists can help you identify and proceed with next steps.

Did You Know?

- 1 of 6 U.S. adults will experience depression at some point in their life - 1/2 of those diagnosed with depression are also diagnosed with an anxiety disorder - The Covid-19 pandemic triggered a 25% increase in anxiety and depression

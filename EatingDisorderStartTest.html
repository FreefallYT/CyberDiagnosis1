Eating Disorder

What is a Eating Disorder?

An eating disorder is a mental illness that has a range of mental conditions that can disturb daily eating habits. They are serious and often fatal conditions that affect an individual's emotions and perception of themselves. Some common eating disorders include bulimia, anorexia, and binge eating.

Is This Accurate?

This online diagnosis test is not 100% accurate compared to a doctor That being said, you can use this as a self-screening tool to track your behaviour and hint towards a possible diagnosis. If you think you have symptoms of an eating disorder, trained medical professionals such as doctors or mental health specialists can help you identify and proceed with next steps.
